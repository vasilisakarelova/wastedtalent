Intro-text: 

Dotan is a Dutch platinum-awarded singer-songwriter and founder of „7 Layers Festival“.
After having played over a hundred living room shows, Dotan finally released his debut album „7 Layers“ in 2014. The album instantly connected with his audience and within a year the Dutch singer-songwriter took the multi-award winning album from living rooms, to international festivals, to his sold out stadium show in Amsterdam. His songs „Home“ and „Hungry“ were instant hits. His music was featured in many popular US television shows and the legendary Ben Folds invited him to tour throughout North America in 2016. 2017 he decided to go back to his own living room and record new music. The song „Bones“ is the first result of a stripped-bare approach going back to his singer-songwriter core.
